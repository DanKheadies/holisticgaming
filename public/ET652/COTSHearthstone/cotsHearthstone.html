Workout #1

Goals

  • Start playing Hearthstone
  • Learn about your cognitive abilities
  • Learn about your meta-cognitive abilities

Whether you're a newb or a veteran, you can develop your mental abilities playing Hearthstone. Let's start by downloading and signing up for Battle.net. If you already have Battle.net and Hearthstone installed, jump to Workout #2.



For the newbies: go to Battle.net and "Get the desktop app for Battle.net".




Blizzard will ask you to enter an account and password, but me thinks you'll need to create one. Go ahead and "Create a Free Account".




Fill out the corresponding information and create the account.




Find the email in your mailbox.




Click the link to verify the account.




And log in with your new account.




Oh yea, accept their terms after you read through it...




Then create a Battle tag. This will be the name that your opponents fear, so choose wisely.




Once you're in, select Hearthstone from Blizzard's list of games.




And let's go ahead and "Play Free Now".




While that's loading, let me explain 2 things to you:


One

This game is a card game. An awesome card game. The basic premise is that you need to understand what your cards can do and what your opponent might be able to do. Think poker but with a LOT more variety and intensity.


Two

You should be thinking a LOT. When I say thinking, I'm talking about cognition. You should be cognitioning a LOT. Cognition means "the ability to think," and cognitive abilities are the mental abilities you use when you're thinking. These abilities include perception, attention, memory, language, problem solving, decision making, and reasoning (just to name a simple few).



So, Hearthstone should feel a little like poker, but it will be engaging a lot more of your cognitive abilities. One last thing before we start playing: meta-cognition. The term meta means "self-referential." In other words, meta-cognition means to think about thinking. Likewise, meta-gaming means to game a game. So as you play Hearthstone, I'm going to challenge you to think about certain cognitive abilities, which is meta-cognition.



Alright, has it finished loading yet? Once you can "Play", go go go... And remember: "when in doubt, click it out." AKA just click anywhere on the screen. Something will / should eventually happen...
